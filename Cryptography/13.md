# 13

## Step by Step Solution
1. Read the challenge — it gives you a scrambled piece of text and hints at something called **ROT13**.
2. ROT13 is a very simple cipher: every letter is just shifted 13 places forward in the alphabet (A becomes N, B becomes O, and so on). Since the alphabet has 26 letters, shifting by 13 twice brings you right back to the original text — which makes it easy to both encode and decode with the same method.
3. Copy the scrambled text from the challenge.
4. Paste it into any online ROT13 tool (like rot13.com) or the ROT13 option in CyberChef.
5. The tool instantly reveals the readable text underneath, including the flag.
